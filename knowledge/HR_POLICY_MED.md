# **HR POLICY: MEDICAL BENEFITS**

## **Page 1: Introduction & Scope**

### **1.1 Purpose**

The purpose of this Medical Benefits Policy is to outline the healthcare benefits provided to employees of the Company. The policy aims to support employee wellbeing, ensure access to medical care, and promote a healthy workforce.

### **1.2 Scope**

This policy applies to:

- All full-time employees
- All part-time employees (pro-rated benefits)
- Contract staff (where applicable in employment agreement)

### **1.3 Objectives**

The Company seeks to:

- Provide affordable healthcare access
- Support preventive care and early treatment
- Reduce financial burden from medical expenses
- Promote long-term employee wellness

### **1.4 Definitions**

- **Employee**: Any individual employed under a valid contract
- **Dependent**: Legal spouse and children under 21 years old
- **Panel Clinic**: Clinics approved by the Company's insurer

## **Page 2: Eligibility & Coverage**
### **2.1 Eligibility Criteria**

#### **Employee Type Eligibility for Benefits**

Full-Time Yes

Part-Time Pro-rated

Contract Staff Case-by-case

### **2.2 Waiting Period**

- New employees are eligible after **3 months of service**
- No waiting period for workplace injury claims

### **2.3 Dependent Coverage**

#### **Dependent Type Coverage**

Spouse Yes

Children (<21) Yes

Parents Optional add-on

### **2.4 Coverage Areas**

The medical benefits include:

- General Practitioner (GP) visits
- Specialist consultations
- Hospitalisation
- Prescription medication
- Preventive health screenings
## **Page 3: Benefits Structure**
### **3.1 Outpatient Benefits**

#### **Benefit Type Coverage Limit (Annual)**

GP Consultation \$500

Specialist Visit \$1,000

Medication Included

### **3.2 Inpatient Benefits**

#### **Item Coverage**

Hospital Stay Up to \$10,000

Surgery Up to \$15,000

ICU Up to \$2,000/day

### **3.3 Co-Payment Structure**

#### **Category Employee Pays Company Pays**

Outpatient 20% 80%

Inpatient 10% 90%

Specialist 30% 70%

### **3.4 Annual Claim Limit**

• Maximum claim per employee: **\$20,000 per year**

## **Page 4: Claims & Procedures**
### **4.1 Panel Clinics**

Employees are encouraged to visit panel clinics to enjoy:

- Cashless treatment
- Lower co-payment

### **4.2 Claim Submission Process**

- 1. Visit clinic or hospital
- 2. Obtain original receipt and medical report
- 3. Submit claim via HR portal within **30 days**
- 4. Claims are processed within **14 working days**

### **4.3 Required Documents**

- Medical receipt
- Doctor's memo
- Prescription (if applicable)

### **4.4 Non-Reimbursable Expenses**

The following are NOT covered:

- Cosmetic procedures
- Non-prescribed supplements
- Experimental treatments
- Self-inflicted injuries

## **Page 5: Special Provisions & Review**
### **5.1 Preventive Care**

Employees are entitled to:

- Annual health screening (up to \$200)
- Vaccinations (company-approved)

### **5.2 Chronic Illness Support**

Employees diagnosed with chronic conditions may receive:

- Extended medication coverage
- Specialist subsidies

### **5.3 Workplace Injury**

All workplace injuries are covered under:

- Work Injury Compensation Insurance
- Full medical expense coverage

### **5.4 Policy Review**

This policy will be reviewed:

- Annually
- Or when regulatory changes occur
